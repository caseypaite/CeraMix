## One-liner

CeraMix turns messy recordings into clean, separated audio — entirely on your own machine.

## Elevator pitch

CeraMix is a cross-platform desktop audio workstation that uses open-source AI
models to clean up recordings, split them into stems (vocals, drums, bass,
other), and remove noise, hiss, and reverb. It wraps best-in-class models —
Demucs v4 / HTDemucs for source separation and DeepFilterNet for speech
enhancement — behind a fast, dark-mode-first interface. Everything runs locally
with hardware acceleration (CoreML / DirectML / CUDA) and a CPU fallback, so
your audio never leaves your device.
